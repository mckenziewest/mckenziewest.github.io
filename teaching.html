Markdown2Canvas

I am a user of the excellent tool Markdown2Canvas, designed by my colleague, silviana amethyst. This tool allows me to keep local markdown versions of my Canvas courses and quickly upload them as needed. Markdown2Canvas is open source and available on GitHub. I highly recommend you give it a try. Oh the time it saves me!
