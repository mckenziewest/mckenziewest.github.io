Mckenzie West - Research

My primary research interests are Arithmetic Geometry, Algebraic Geometry and Number Thoery. My dissertation advisor was David Zureick-Brown.

Rethinking Number Theory

I have co-organized three Rethinking Number Theory workshops. If you're interested in learning about future RNT workshops or organizing your own Rethinking workshop please reach out to me (westmr at uwec dot edu) or to the RNT team (Rethinking.Number.Theory at gmail dot com).

Publications

  • Minimum Distance and Parameter Ranges of Locally Recoverable Codes with Availability from Fiber Products of Curves, with M. Chara, S. Kottler, B. Malmskog, and B. Thompson. (Designs, Codes and Cryptography (2023)); published version.
  • Restrictions on Weil polynomials of Jacobians of hyperelliptic curves, with E. Costa, R. Donepudi, R. Fernando, V. Karemaker, and C. Springer. (Arithmetic Geometry, Number Theory, and Computation (2021), pp. 259-276); arXiv, published version.
